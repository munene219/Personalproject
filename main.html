My ataraxia💕(najua hujai skia io👀),

Before you came into my world, it was a blank canvas,but not the kind full of possibility. It was dark. Fear hidden in the cracks of the walls, uncertainty spread everywhere. I was clinging on to remains of my former self, a painting left unfinished and fading.

Then you became the sun in my world, a reason to smile.😊 You gave me love💓something that I had missed and which lacked in my life. You painted your way into my heart with every smile, every laugh, every moment. You always see the good in me and push me to be better😭. I am more than grateful.🥹
